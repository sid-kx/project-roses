Policy

Order & Care Policy

Every bouquet is handmade to order, so we keep our policy clear and simple. Please read through the details below before placing an order so you know exactly how deposits, changes, delivery and pickups work.

Quick Policy Highlights

  • Handmade to order Each bouquet is custom-made, so all orders are final once confirmed.
  • Order timing We recommend placing your order at least 5–7 days before your event.
  • Changes & edits Small changes are allowed within 24 hours of order confirmation.
  • Delivery & pickup Pickup is near The Gore Rd & Ebenezer Dr. Limited local delivery is available for nearby areas.
  • Payment A 50% e-transfer deposit is required before we start your bouquet. The remaining balance is due at pickup or before delivery, and bouquets must be picked up within one week of being ready.
  • Care Keep bouquets away from water, direct sun and open flames to maintain their shape and colour.

How Orders Work

When you submit a custom order form, we review your colours, add-ons and details and then reach out by call to confirm everything. Once the design and price are confirmed with you, we require a 50% deposit by e-transfer before we start making your bouquet and your order is considered booked. After your bouquet is finished, it must be picked up within one week of the time we let you know it is ready. Because every bouquet is handmade and materials are prepared specifically for you, orders become final at this stage.

Timing, Changes & Cancellations

To make sure your bouquet is ready on time, we recommend placing your order 5–7 days before you need it. Small changes, like switching a ribbon shade or add-on, can usually be made within 24 hours of confirming your order, depending on material availability. If you need to cancel, please let us know as soon as possible. Because materials are purchased per order, deposits and payments may not be refundable once work has started.

Delivery & Pickup

Pickup is from a safe, public location near The Gore Rd & Ebenezer Dr in Brampton. For some nearby areas, the florist may be able to offer local delivery, depending on schedule and distance. If delivery is available for your order, we will confirm the exact time, location and any delivery fee with you during the chat after you submit your form. Please make sure someone is available to receive the bouquet at the agreed time.

Custom Requests & Colours

We do our best to match your inspo photos, favourite colours and themes using the ribbons and add-ons we have available. Exact shades, characters and wraps may vary slightly from reference photos, but the overall vibe and style of the bouquet will always be kept. If something requested is not possible, we will suggest the closest alternative that still fits your occasion and budget.

Care & Longevity

Ribbon bouquets are designed to be long-lasting keepsakes. To keep them looking their best, store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight, water and humidity. Avoid crushing or pressing the bouquet, and keep it away from candles or open flames. With gentle handling, your Roses by Aikam bouquet can stay on display for months or even years.
