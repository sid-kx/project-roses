About

About Roses by Aikam

Roses by Aikam started with a simple idea – to create gifts that last longer than real flowers and feel as special as the moment they're given. Every bouquet is handcrafted in Brampton, with carefully chosen ribbons, colours and add-ons made just for you.

Whether it's a birthday, anniversary, proposal or a "just because" surprise, each bouquet is made to match your person's style and story, from ribbon shades to the final wrap.

Handmade in Brampton

Every bouquet is crafted by hand – no mass production, just careful, neat work.

Fully Customizable

Choose ribbon colours, names, messages, butterflies, glitter and Dior-style wrapping.

Long-Lasting Gifts

Ribbon roses that don't wilt – a keepsake they can keep on their shelf for years.

For Every Occasion
